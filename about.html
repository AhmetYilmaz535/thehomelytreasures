About Us

The Homely Treasures LLC is a technology and e-commerce enterprise registered in the United States. We operate at the intersection of digital software development and modern commerce.

Company Background

Founded as a dynamic LLC in the US, our core objective is to deliver value through digital innovation. We maintain a steadfast commitment to professional standards, ensuring compliance and quality across all our operational domains.

Our Business Scope

Our business activities are primarily categorized into two main pillars:

  • Technology & Software Development: We engineer robust mobile applications and software systems designed for a variety of platforms including Apple's iOS and Google's Android ecosystems. We handle the complete software lifecycle from initial architecture to final deployment.
  • Digital & Physical Commerce: Leveraging our technological infrastructure, we actively participate in e-commerce markets, managing retail operations, supply chains, and customer fulfillment with a focus on efficiency.

Corporate Responsibility

As a registered business entity, we take our corporate and legal responsibilities seriously. We strictly adhere to the guidelines set forth by digital distribution platforms and national business regulations, ensuring a transparent and trustworthy relationship with our partners, platforms, and end-users.
